About Us

An unforgettable circus experience

The Wild Circus is a group of wild artists and animals, playing and having fun since 1999. First settled by the Wildcode family in the forest of Nowhere, they were quickly joined by friends and artists willing to practise their art in unexpected places.

The Show

Animals
We bring to you the most astonishing wild wonders that nature gave us ! Come and get thrilled among wonderful animals, playing free in the city !

High-Wire
After walking over the Grand Canyon, the Niagara Falls and the Yosemite Park, our 5 world-record wild-high-wire artist, is now flying over your city ! Come and see him walk over your head !

Fire-juggling
Be dazzled by the magic of our fire-juggling artists ! Their fire-sculptures bring stars, moons and planets down on Earth, so close, that you can almost touch the Universe...
